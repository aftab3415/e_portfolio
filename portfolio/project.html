Home Services On Demand

This project was developed during the 5th semester of my final year degree in 2021. It was developed in a team of 2 members.

When a person requires any services like Plumbing, Electrical, and Electronics, Pest Control, Home Paint, Cleaning and Disinfection, Carpentry, etc. then, a personal meeting or a phone call is set up to place an appointment with the service providers to solve the issue.

This is a tedious and time-consuming process and is difficult for a person to opt for any of these services in case of emergency at any place or time.

Hence, this project is designed to cater to the needs of people by providing services for online booking of service providers.

I worked on this project for 6 months.

Technologies:

  • - Django
  • - HTML
  • - CSS
  • - JAVASCRIPT
